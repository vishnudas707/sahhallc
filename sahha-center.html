Sahha LLC's State-of-the-Art Wound Care & Oxygen Therapy Center

The Sahha LLC state-of-the-art center is located in Dubai's Healthcare City. The clinic features many advanced treatment options including topical wound oxygen therapy (TWO2), revascularization, bioengineered skin substitutes, compression therapy, negative pressure wound therapy and debridement. In addition to diabetic wounds, the center is able to treat any type of skin ulcers, including pressure ulcers, arterial and venous stasis ulcers, non-healing surgical wounds, complex soft tissue ulcers and traumatic wounds.

Using a comprehensive interdisciplinary approach to diagnosis, treatment and prevention, we offer personalized care to Emiratis in a comforting, state-of-the-art outpatient facility. Here, patients can rest assured that they have professional partners committed to providing gold-standard care, and who understand their unique health and healing concerns.

American Spine Center
